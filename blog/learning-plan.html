My learning plan for Dev Academy

What is my long-term goal or career pathway?

My long-term goal is to have the freedom of being able to work from anywhere. With that freedom, I want to move to a zero-tax country because who would not want 40% extra pay? I would like to go down a career path in crypto or web3 gaming (video games made through the blockchain). The benefits of working for these companies allow for a lot more money to be made since they offer stock options and coin tokens alongside salary pay.

A description of my strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

My strengths are that I am good at not procrastinating. I like to consistently do a task given to me throughout the week rather than draining myself for only two days to get something done. Another strength of mine for learning especially in the Dev Academy environment (online) is that I have taught myself HTML and CSS to an advanced level by watching videos and practicing so I know what learning method works best for me, that being practical learning by actually writing the code as I follow along with a video.

A limitation I have when it comes to learning is that my mind can think about things I am looking forward to after I finish a task which then makes me unfocused so I have to track back a few minutes in a tutorial since I wasn’t focusing and didn’t comprehend what the section was about.

What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would I like to see developed in myself while at Dev Academy?

I would like to be more assertive in my ideas. Whenever I believe to have a good idea I tend to get scared that others will think it is a bad idea and won’t work. I think I can generally improve a project like in school or a leadership situation but I tend to back away since I'm afraid to speak up.

A commitment to how I will manage my workload in this program.

I am managing my workload by giving myself a period where I learn on this course every day, 9am-5pm. I don’t have any commitments besides keeping myself fit with Muay Thai, so I have plenty of time to work throughout this program.

A way I will work with other learners and facilitators will be through communication through the discord by either chat or video call. I have already asked my home group some questions and the other students and facilitator have helped me in answering these questions. They have also provided feedback on some work that I sent in which helped me in making my blog website better.

When times are getting tough it is easiest for me to just take a break to let my mind think about the problem in a different environment than being in front of a screen. I might go to the gym or spend some time outside on a walk or run to give myself a reboot before I get back into the heavy workload.

A commitment as to how and where I will seek help in a timely way

I will use the discord chat as a way to seek help or chat GPT. Chat GPT is a quick way for an easy solution to code and any technical questions I may have. The discord I will use for more course-related questions may be about the technical side of what the course is instructing me to do and for help about the course since the facilitators and even some students will have to answer for me.

Schedule:

A commitment I am making time around is Muay Thai. I love the sport, it keeps me fit, and strong and helps with my anxiety and stress. I tend to have Muay Thai sessions most days throughout the week but it starts at a reasonable time of 5:30 pm or 6:30 pm so I have most of the day to work on the course content.
