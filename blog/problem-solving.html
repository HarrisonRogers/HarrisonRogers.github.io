When I was stuck on a problem

A simple problem I faced was during the whereAreYou.js challenge where I was stuck trying to output “Scooby” on the computer. I had to go through an array of objects to find out which object Scooby was hiding in. I had the code right but I forgot one simple step of returning the function.

To help me solve this problem though I took a break from coding to let myself re-gather and take my mind off of the challenge, I also asked questions in the tech questions which helped narrow down that it was something to do with the return method. These methods helped me out a lot because when faced with this challenge I was getting frustrated and angry that it didn’t work, but these strategies made me take my mind off of the challenge and re-gather my thoughts.

I learnt a valuable lesson about reaching out for help no matter the difficulty of the challenge because in the end, I am paying for this course so the facilitators should help me out. I also learned about the placement of the return statement when dealing with functions.
